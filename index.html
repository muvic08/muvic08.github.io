About Us

Chepnet.org is a community not-for-profit based in Bomet and Narok counties, Kenya. Our mission is to advance the proficiency of rural women in technology and to invite and inspire more girls to pursue careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).

Challenge

Because they live in rural, disadvantaged, underserved communities, the women we work with face many challenges, including illiteracy, lack of business expertise, and lack of facilities for any kind of work training. Technology will enable them to stay on top of current affairs, enrich their social lives, stay in touch with family, have access to entertainment, respond to climatic changes, educate themselves on child health care, and otherwise improve quality of life for themselves and their families. It also makes them competitive applicants to the increasing number of technology-based job openings throughout Africa.

Solution

We work with women because women are underrepresented in STEM fields both locally and worldwide, and because we know that to invest in a woman is to invest in her whole community. Through the training we provide, we will create a pool of professional women that can pay it forward through mentoring schoolgirls and inspiring them to pursue STEM fields in turn.
